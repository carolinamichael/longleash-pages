Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

LongLeash is an independently developed app, not a company product — this policy is written in plain language on purpose, because the actual privacy story here is simple: your data stays on your phone.

What LongLeash collects

LongLeash stores the following, entirely on your device, using Apple's own on-device storage:

  • Your dog's name, breed, age, and photos you choose to add
  • A voice recording, and optionally a video, of your dog that you record yourself
  • Your walk history — routes, duration, distance, ratings, and favorites
  • Any calendar events LongLeash creates on your behalf (walk invites you've approved)

None of this is ever uploaded to a server, sold, shared, or made available to the developer, advertisers, or any other third party. There is no user account, no sign-in, and no backend server that LongLeash talks to for any of the above. If you delete the app, this data is gone — there's nothing stored anywhere else to delete.

The one exception: weather

To suggest good walk times, LongLeash checks the weather using Apple's own WeatherKit service. This sends your approximate location (a coordinate) to Apple in order to get a forecast back — it does not send your name, your dog's info, or anything else about you. This exchange is governed by Apple's own privacy policies, not LongLeash's.

You can decline any of these in iOS Settings at any time; LongLeash simply won't be able to offer the related feature.

No tracking, no ads, no analytics

LongLeash contains no advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics or tracking libraries, and does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because it doesn't track you across other apps or websites.

Children's privacy

LongLeash is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Deleting your data

Since everything lives only on your device, deleting the app deletes everything. LongLeash's Settings screen also includes a "Reset all app data" option if you'd like to start fresh without deleting the app itself.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future feature like optional iCloud backup is added — this page will be updated with a new date at the top.

A note on liability

LongLeash is provided as an independent, hobby-developed app, as-is, without warranty of any kind. Walk suggestions, weather information, and route recommendations are provided for convenience only — always use your own judgment about weather, traffic, and surroundings when walking your dog.
