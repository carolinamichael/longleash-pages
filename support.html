Common questions

Does LongLeash need an internet connection?

Mostly no — almost everything runs entirely on your device. The one exception is checking the weather, which briefly connects to Apple's own weather service.

Where is my dog's data stored?

Only on your phone. LongLeash doesn't have a server, an account system, or any way to see your data — see the Privacy Policy for details.

I switched phones / reinstalled the app and lost my dog's info — can I get it back?

Not currently — since everything is stored only on-device by design, there's no backup today. (Optional iCloud backup is a planned future feature.)

How do I reset the app / start over?

Settings → "Reset all app data."
